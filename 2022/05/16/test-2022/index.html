Introduce Snow

2022-05-16
#Dog
Introduction

I’d like to introduce our new puppy, Snow.

If you don’t know about me, I live with my wife in Virginia. We first adopted a husky back in Dec 2019. His name is Dodo(嘟嘟). He’s a sweet boy.

By the time Dodo turned three years old. We started considering getting him a sister. We were so excited about our new family member even though we didn’t know who she’s gonna be. We would like to have a white puppy and name her Snow. So we can say, “You know nothing, Zhang Snow.” By the way, my last name is Zhang, pronounces similar to John. Her nick name would be Dandan(蛋蛋), which means egg or new life in Chinese. Dodo and Dandan sound like a family, don’t they?

We didn’t proactively look for Dandan, because we know she would come to us. We believe in destiny. One day, I saw someone posted on social media that seven husky puppies were looking for new homes, and two of them are pure white girls. I knew Dandan was among them. We scheduled a visit for that weekend right away.

On Dec 5th 2021, we went to the puppies home with excitement. Dodo was with us, he wanted to be part of this big decision. We were particularly interested in those two white puppies. Unfortunately, one was already reserved. When we approached to the other one, she made an unfriendly growling in the throat. We started to question ourselves. We really wanted a white puppy, that was our plan. But this puppy didn’t seem like to go home with us. We ended up choosing a brown and white girl. She was the first one who came to us when we entered the house. We believe in destiny. Also, she was the most calm one among the puppies. Since we already had a dog in our home, we wanted them to get along with each other.

Dandan was so tiny then, only 7 weeks old and 9 pounds. She was so delicate, with beautiful eyes and soft fur. She was curious about everything in this world. She sniffed, she barked, she jumped. She tried everything she can to prove her presense in this world. She knew this would be her new home.

Dandan was welcomed by all the neighbours. The uncle lives across our door said, “Look, family is growing!” When I walked her ourside, someone asked, “How much?” I didn’t get it at first time. I said, “9 pounds” I thought he was asking how much weight. He then clarified, “How much money?” Later I found out that was the way Americans would use to express their love to the puppy, because another guy asked me the same question. And he was willing to pay 50 more bucks to buy her. I knew he was just joking.

Dandan was very shy for the first several days. When walking by other persons or dogs, she would hide behind me. She even hid below Dodo’s belly when she first heard the sound of a squirrel climbing the tree. Dodo was like, “No worries, it’s just squirrels. They are fun to chase after.” Such a sweet boy and caring brother. Later Dandan became more and more socialized, she likes playing hunting games with Dodo, she would just lay down and let people rub her soft belly.

Thanks for reading the story of Dandan. Later I will tell more about how we dealt with this trouble maker and what was this drama queen like.
