Elon Musk Talk About Wechat

2022-05-18
Translation

For those who have used Wechat, I think Wechat is actually a good model. If you are in China, it’s basically you kind of living on Wechat. It does everything. It’s sort of like Twitter, plus Paypal, plus a whole bunch of other things, and all roll into one with a great interface. It’s really an excellent app. We don’t have anything like that outside of China.

Such an app would be really useful. It’s just like the utility of a spam free thing where you can make comments, you can post videos. I think it’s important for content creators to have a revenue share. This does not need to be done on Twitter. It could be done from something that’s created from scratch. It could be somthing new. But I think this thing needs to exist, whether it is converting Twitter to be the sort of like kind of all-compassing app that like said everything from digital town square where important ideas are debated, you know maximally trusted and inclusive and at a point where you sort of have a high trust situation then payments whether it’s crytpo or fiat can make a lot of sense. Just want something that’s incredibly useful and that people love using. It’s either convert Twitter to that or start something new. It needs to happen somehow.
